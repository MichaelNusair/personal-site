# Founder Portfolio — Michael Nusair

## 10+ Ventures Built as Solo CTO

Most engineers talk about side projects. Michael builds companies.

Between 2021 and 2025 — while holding a full-time engineering position at a defense technology company — Michael designed, built, and launched **10+ technology products** as the sole technical founder.

---

## The Venture Mindset

Michael approaches ventures the way a special forces operator approaches a mission: identify the objective, plan the architecture, execute fast, and iterate based on real-world feedback. Every venture follows the same discipline:

1. **Identify a real problem** — not a cool technology looking for a use case
2. **Design the minimum viable architecture** — enough structure to scale, not so much that it slows down learning
3. **Ship fast** — get to users in weeks, not months
4. **Measure and iterate** — A/B test, analyze, evolve

## What This Demonstrates

### Technical Range
These ventures span **7 programming languages**, **3 cloud providers**, and architectural patterns from serverless functions to DDS-based distributed simulations. Michael isn't locked into one stack — he picks the right tool for the job.

### Full Ownership
Every venture required Michael to handle:
- System architecture and design
- Frontend and backend development
- Database modeling and optimization
- Cloud infrastructure and deployment
- CI/CD pipeline setup
- Payment integration (Stripe)
- Authentication systems
- Monitoring and operations

### Speed
These were built during nights and weekends alongside a demanding full-time role. Michael knows how to ship fast because he has to — his time is his most constrained resource.

### Business Acumen
These aren't just code repositories. They're products with:
- User-facing features and workflows
- A/B testing frameworks
- Analytics and tracking
- Payment processing
- Authentication and authorization
